The Flow
How It Works
From nomination to ceremony — fully automated.
1

Create Your Program

Admin creates a new award program — custom name, branding, and ceremony date. Takes 2 minutes. Runs completely independently from any other program on the platform.

2

Set Your Categories

Choose from the standard category library or create fully custom ones. Each category gets its own nomination pool and vote tally. Categories are independent per program.

3

Nominees Sign Up & Get Their Link

Podcasters register, select the categories they want to compete in, and instantly receive a unique shareable voting URL. They paste it on Instagram, X, Facebook — wherever their audience lives.

4

Fans Vote

Voters land on the nominee's branded page, see every category they're in, and vote with one click. Up to 3 votes per category. Nominees can vote for themselves. Fully mobile-optimized.

5

Watch the Leaderboard Live

Admin sees real-time vote counts, 1st/2nd/3rd place rankings per category, voter details, and can export everything to CSV. Refreshes every 15 seconds automatically.

6

Nominees Track Their Rank

Every podcaster can see exactly where they stand in their dashboard — "You are ranked #2 of 8 nominees in Best Interview Show." Drives them to keep sharing their voting link.
